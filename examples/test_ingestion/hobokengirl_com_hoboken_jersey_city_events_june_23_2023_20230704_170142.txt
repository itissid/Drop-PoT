Center for Dermatology + Skin Surgery’s June Promotion 
Available until June 30th    |    BOOK AN APPOINTMENT (https://centerfordermatologynj.com/contact-us/) 
Center for Dermatology and Skin Surgery, located at 129 Washington Street, Suite 100 in Hoboken and 1 W. Ridgewood Avenue, Suite 103 in Paramus, specializes in medical, surgical, and cosmetic dermatology. The team is focused on caring for patients with a wide variety of skincare needs for all patients. From now until June 30th, you can receive 15% off all neurotoxin + filler injectables and 20% off all products when you mention ‘Hoboken Girl’. 
Click here to book an appointment (https://centerfordermatologynj.com/contact-us/)    and click    here (https://centerfordermatologynj.com/)    to learn more about the Center for Dermatology + Skin Surgery.

The Community Food Bank of New Jersey (https://cfbnj.org/)  | Hillside 
The Community FoodBank of New Jersey, a member of Feeding America®, is the leading anti-hunger + anti-poverty organization in the state. What started as a modest enterprise in the back of founder Kathleen DiChiara’s station wagon in downtown Newark in 1975 became an incorporated non-profit in 1982 and has grown to two warehouse facilities in Hillside + Egg Harbor Township.    Learn more  here (https://cfbnj.org/) .
